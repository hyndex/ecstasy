Hello There!

Ecstasy is the annual cultural fest of Central Institute of Technology, Kokrajhar, Assam. Being one of the largest cultural fest in entire lower Assam which witnessed various national and international artists. Spread over 3 days and 3 nights, Every year it is conducted at the end of February. Ecstasy has over 40 competitions. The coming edition of Ecstasy is strives to be greatest till date. It is scheduled to be held from 12th Mar to 14th Mar 2020. Thank you.

Ecstasy 2020 Featuring 

Ecstasy has over 40 competitions. Some of them are voice of Ecstasy, Mr & Miss Ecstasy, The bridal competitions is another beauty pageant competition of Ecstasy.
